- **Systemic Elegance**: It eliminates entire debates, like whether prisoners should work, vote, or access welfare, by deferring to external norms. This could simplify administration, reduce legal challenges, and foster a more humane environment, potentially lowering recidivism through maintained social ties and economic skills.
