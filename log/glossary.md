# Glossary: Civic Mandate Republic

**Chancellor** — Elected, limited-term executive for diplomacy, emergencies, and ceremony under retroactive audits.  
**Civic Intelligence Office (CIO)** — Models and communicates mandates; creates accessible briefings and simulations.  
**Metrics Bureau** — Independent auditing body; maintains Civic Outcomes Ledger.  
**Civic Outcomes Ledger** — Public, versioned repository of all mandates, metrics, and performance data.  
**Public Mandates** — Citizen-approved societal goals replacing legislative proposals.  
**Foundational Mandates** — Core, ongoing state responsibilities (defense, justice, infrastructure).  
**Strategic Mandates** — Adaptive, time-limited societal goals reviewed cyclically.  
**Policycraft** — Professional discipline of policy design, simulation, and evaluation.  
**Civic Funding Projects (CFPs)** — Citizen-initiated pilots aligned to Mandates.  
**Externality Pricing Act** — Tax framework for negative externalities with progressive rebate.  
**Civic Mandate Economy Act** — Governing act defining Mandates, Metrics, CIO, and Policycraft systems.  
**30/60/90 Rule** — Retroactive executive disclosure protocol.  
**Chain-of-Density Summary** — Concise narrative capturing cumulative reasoning with minimal redundancy.
