# Project Index: The Civic Mandate Constitution

## Purpose
An experimental exploration of governance designed around the assumptions of human self-interest, corruption resilience, and incentive alignment.

This repository contains the evolving “Civic Mandate Republic” constitution, supporting acts, and a complete design log capturing every insight, tradeoff, and superseded idea for historical and AI reconstruction.

---

## Structure

- `/constitution.md` — Base document of principles.
- `/acts/` — Specific policy frameworks (Externality Pricing, Civic Mandate Economy).
- `/log/` — Intellectual record of the design process.
  - `/ideas/` — All decision records and superseded reasoning.
  - `/summary/` — Compressed Chain-of-Density summaries.
  - `/changelog.md` — Chronological evolution.
  - `/glossary.md` — Canonical terminology.
  - `/open-questions.md` — Unresolved issues and areas of exploration.

All content is version-controlled and fully traceable.
